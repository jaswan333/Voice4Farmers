CROP NAME: Tomato (Solanum lycopersicum)

GENERAL INFORMATION:
Tomato is a widely cultivated vegetable crop.
Ideal temperature: 18°C to 30°C.
Soil: Well-drained sandy loam with pH 6.0–7.5.

POPULAR VARIETIES:
- Pusa Ruby
- Arka Rakshak
- Arka Vikas
- PKM 1

SEED TREATMENT:
Treat seeds with Trichoderma 4g per kg seed before sowing.

FERTILIZER RECOMMENDATION PER ACRE:
- Farm Yard Manure: 8–10 tons
- Urea: 50 kg
- DAP: 100 kg
- Potash: 50 kg

COMMON DISEASES:

1. EARLY BLIGHT:
Symptoms:
- Brown concentric rings on leaves.
- Yellowing and leaf drop.

Treatment:
- Spray Mancozeb 2 g per liter.
- Remove infected leaves.

2. LATE BLIGHT:
Symptoms:
- Water-soaked lesions.
- White fungal growth under leaves.

Treatment:
- Spray Metalaxyl 1 g per liter.
- Avoid overhead irrigation.

3. LEAF CURL VIRUS:
Symptoms:
- Leaf curling.
- Stunted growth.

Cause:
Whitefly transmission.

Treatment:
- Spray Imidacloprid 0.3 ml per liter.
- Use yellow sticky traps.

PEST MANAGEMENT:

1. FRUIT BORER:
Symptoms:
- Holes in fruits.
- Larvae inside fruit.

Treatment:
- Spray Spinosad 0.5 ml per liter.
- Install pheromone traps.

IRRIGATION:
Irrigate every 5–7 days.
Avoid waterlogging.

HARVEST:
Harvest fruits at mature green or red stage.
