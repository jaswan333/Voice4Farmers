CROP NAME: Cotton (Gossypium spp.)

GENERAL INFORMATION:
Cotton is a major fiber crop.
Ideal temperature: 21°C to 30°C.
Soil: Black cotton soil with good drainage.

POPULAR VARIETIES:
- Bt Cotton
- MCU 5
- LRA 5166
- RCH 2

SEED TREATMENT:
Treat seeds with Imidacloprid 5 ml per kg seed to prevent sucking pests.

FERTILIZER RECOMMENDATION PER ACRE:
- Urea: 60 kg
- DAP: 50 kg
- Potash: 25 kg

COMMON PESTS:

1. PINK BOLLWORM:
Symptoms:
- Rosette flowers.
- Damaged bolls.

Treatment:
- Install pheromone traps.
- Spray Emamectin Benzoate 0.4 g per liter.

2. WHITEFLY:
Symptoms:
- Yellowing leaves.
- Sticky honeydew secretion.

Treatment:
- Spray Thiamethoxam 0.3 g per liter.
- Avoid excessive nitrogen.

3. APHIDS:
Symptoms:
- Curled leaves.
- Sticky surface on leaves.

Treatment:
- Spray Neem oil 5 ml per liter.

DISEASES:

1. WILT:
Symptoms:
- Sudden plant drying.
- Brown discoloration in stem.

Treatment:
- Crop rotation.
- Use resistant varieties.

IRRIGATION:
Irrigate every 10–15 days depending on soil moisture.

HARVEST:
Pick bolls when fully opened and dry.
